Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cost to attend?

There is no charge to attend. Complimentary wine and light refreshments are provided as our guests. There is no obligation to retain our firm.

Can I bring my spouse or partner?

Absolutely — we encourage it. Estate planning decisions affect the whole household, and couples often find the conversation most productive together.

Do I need to have any documents prepared beforehand?

No preparation is required. Just bring yourself and any questions you have. We'll provide a brief pre-event questionnaire if you'd like to think ahead.

What topics are covered?

Each event has a featured topic (noted above), but all sessions cover core estate planning: wills, revocable living trusts, powers of attorney, healthcare directives, and beneficiary designations.

What happens after the event?

Guests are invited to schedule a complimentary 30-minute consultation with a DeCosimo Law attorney to discuss their specific situation — completely at their discretion.
